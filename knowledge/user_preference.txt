PRODUCT: hq0 (https://hq0.com)

WHAT IT IS:
A branded video meeting platform. Teams host client calls on their own domain
(e.g. meet.yourcompany.com) instead of generic Zoom or Google Meet links.

CORE DIFFERENTIATORS:
- No bot joins the call to take notes — recording and transcription happen inside
  the room, invisibly. No awkward "Fireflies.ai is recording this meeting" notification.
- Automated AI summaries and follow-up emails sent from your domain/email, not
  a generic noreply address
- Custom domains, logos, and brand colors across the entire meeting experience
- White-label option (~$15/mo add-on) — removes all hq0 branding
- Free to start (no credit card required)

KEY FEATURES:
- Host meetings on your domain (meet.yourcompany.com)
- Branded meeting links (e.g., /sales-demo instead of /abc-xyz-123)
- Bot-free recording — recording happens inside the room
- AI-generated meeting summaries (GPT-4 powered, automatic)
- Automated follow-up emails from your own email + domain
- Guest onboarding email sequences (Pro, $19/user/mo)
- Real-time in-meeting chat (Pro, $5/user/mo)
- Multilingual subtitles and dubbing (Enterprise)
- Team analytics — engagement visibility across the team
- CRM integration (Salesforce, HubSpot, Pipedrive) — auto-log meetings
- Slack — meeting notifications and AI summaries in your channels
- Stripe — accept payments directly from the meeting platform
- Calendar sync (Google Calendar + Outlook, two-way)
- Zapier — 5,000+ app connections
- SAML SSO (Okta, Auth0) — Enterprise

TARGET CUSTOMERS:
- Sales teams (especially teams doing customer-facing demos and onboarding calls)
- Customer success teams
- Recruiters
- Consultants and account managers
- Any team where client meetings are high-stakes and brand perception matters

COMPETITIVE POSITIONING:
vs Google Meet / Zoom: Your brand on every call, not theirs. AI features free (not
locked behind expensive plans). Post-meeting follow-up is automated.
vs Fireflies/Otter/Fathom: No bot as a visible participant, no client consent issue,
no third-party server storing your client conversations.

BRAND VOICE FOR OUTREACH:
- Warm, direct, casual — like a smart teammate, not a marketer
- Never: "I hope this finds you well", "I wanted to reach out", "exciting updates!"
- Never: bullet lists in emails, generic CTAs, clickbait subject lines
- Always: specific callbacks to what was discussed, flowing prose, one soft question at the end
- Emails should be max 5 sentences in the body
